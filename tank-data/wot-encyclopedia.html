Use Tankopedia to find out more about characteristics of various vehicles featured in the game. You can browse vehicles by nation or by tier, explore basic and advanced configurations. Each article includes all guns, turrets, engines, radios and suspension available for a specific vehicle with detailed characteristics of each module.

Tankopedia contains useful information on World of Tanks vehicles for both new and experienced players.
